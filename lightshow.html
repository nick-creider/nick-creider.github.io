Holiday Light Shows

Since 2014, I have been constructing, programming, and hosting a synchronized holiday light display every December. The controllers for the lights come in kits that you solder together and then program with a computer. As our neighbors, friends, and family drive past, they can tune their car radios to our FM station that I broadcast with a low power transmitter to hear the lights dance to the music.

Progressively, the show has gotten more and more elaborate. Last year's display featured a 21' tall megatree, 7 smaller trees, and a projection mapped video segment in one of the windows.
