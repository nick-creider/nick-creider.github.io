About Me

For as long as I can remember, I've been drawn to the magic of storytelling through immersive, themed experiences.

Since then, my passion has been to help bring these kinds of experiences to others through various mediums.

I've condensed a lot of my work and had to exclude some for this portfolio, but if you'd like to know more, please reach out!

Skills

Creative Development

At the heart of every good concept is a great story. I love helping craft experiences that place guests in times and places otherwise long gone, straight out of the pages of a fairy tale, in the wonders of tomorrow, or anything in between.

Electrical Show Systems

I've been a tinkerer my whole life and because of that, I've come to work with and implement many low-voltage electrical systems and gained a working competency of PLC-based industrial control systems

Lighting & Sound Design

Projection mapping, DMX lighting, attraction sound mixing, synchronized musical light displays, networked audio, oh my!

Fabrication & Finishing

There are few things in this world more satisfying than a good faux finish. Unfortunately to get to the finishing stage, you have to build whatever you're going to paint; I can weld, work with wood, and design in CAD for production.
